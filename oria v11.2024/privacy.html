Privacy Policy

Your privacy is our top priority. Learn how we protect and manage your data.

This Privacy Policy explains how Oria AI Assistant ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects your personal information. By using our service, you trust us with your information, and we are committed to maintaining that trust. Please read this policy carefully to understand our practices regarding your data.

Last updated: March 2025

securityData Collection

We collect and store the following information to provide and improve our services:

  • Account information (username and email) - Required for account creation and service personalization
  • Chat history (stored locally on your device) - To provide conversation continuity and improve user experience
  • Usage statistics (anonymized) - For service improvement and feature optimization
  • Device information - To optimize performance and ensure compatibility
  • Feedback and support requests - To assist you and improve our services
  • Technical logs - For security and troubleshooting purposes
  • Cookies and local storage data - For maintaining your preferences and session information

lockData Security

Your privacy and data security are our top priorities. We implement the following comprehensive security measures:

  • End-to-end encryption for all communications using industry-standard protocols
  • Local storage of sensitive information with secure encryption
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • Secure data transmission using SSL/TLS encryption
  • Limited data retention periods with automatic deletion
  • Access controls and authentication mechanisms
  • Regular security updates and patch management
  • Employee training on data protection and security best practices

shareData Sharing

We maintain strict controls over your personal information and do not share it with third parties, except in the following limited circumstances:

  • When required by law or legal process
  • To protect our rights, privacy, safety, or property
  • With your explicit consent and authorization
  • For essential service operations with trusted partners
  • In the event of a merger, acquisition, or business transfer
  • To prevent fraud or ensure platform safety
  • With service providers under strict confidentiality agreements

cookieCookies & Storage

We use local storage and cookies for the following purposes:

  • Remember your preferences and settings
  • Maintain chat history locally for better user experience
  • Improve service performance and reliability
  • Store theme settings and customizations
  • Keep you logged in securely
  • Analyze usage patterns for service improvement

You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences, but this may affect some features.

drawTools & Ideas Data

How we handle data in the Tools and Ideas feature:

  • Ideas and notes are stored locally in your browser's storage
  • We do not collect or store your ideas on our servers
  • Tags and categories are saved locally with your ideas
  • Sharing functionality uses standard sharing APIs of your device
  • Deleted ideas are permanently removed from local storage
  • We recommend not storing sensitive personal information in ideas
  • Backup and export features are provided for data portability
  • Local storage can be cleared through your browser settings

exploreDiscover Data Usage

Information about data handling in the Discover section:

  • Interaction data is anonymized for analytics purposes
  • Content preferences may be stored locally for personalization
  • Third-party content links are clearly marked

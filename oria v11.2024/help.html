help Frequently Asked Questions

What can Oria help me with? expand_more

Oria can assist you with a wide range of tasks including:

  • Answering questions and explaining concepts
  • Helping with coding problems and debugging
  • Generating creative content
  • Analyzing images and providing insights
  • Offering learning resources and tutorials
  • Providing step-by-step guidance
How do I get the best responses from Oria? expand_more

To get the most helpful responses:

  • Be specific in your questions
  • Provide relevant context
  • Break complex problems into smaller parts
  • Use follow-up questions to clarify
  • Share code snippets or screenshots when discussing technical issues
  • Mention your preferred programming language when asking coding questions
What are the different versions of Oria? expand_more

Oria comes in three versions:

  • XSS 4Q (Beta): Latest version with advanced features, ultra-fast responses, and image analysis
  • XQS 2S (Stable): Reliable version for general use with consistent performance
  • XQS 4S (Coming Soon): Premium version combining speed with enhanced capabilities

You can switch versions in the settings menu to find what works best for you.

How do I use the image analysis feature? expand_more

To analyze images with Oria:

  1. Click the attachment button in the chat input
  2. Select an image file from your device
  3. Wait for the image to upload
  4. Ask questions about the image or request analysis

Note: Image analysis is available in XSS 4Q and XQS 4S versions.

How do I customize my Oria experience? expand_more

You can customize Oria through the settings menu:

  • Toggle between light and dark themes
  • Enable/disable voice responses
  • Switch between different versions
  • Update your profile information
  • Adjust notification preferences

Access settings through the gear icon in the side panel.

How do I provide feedback or report issues? expand_more

To send feedback or report issues:

  1. Open the side panel menu
  2. Click the "Send Feedback" button
  3. Choose your feedback type
  4. Rate your experience
  5. Describe your feedback or issue
  6. Provide contact information (optional)
  7. Submit the form

Our team reviews all feedback to improve Oria.

How do I save and export my conversations? expand_more

To manage your conversations:

  • Conversations are automatically saved locally
  • Use the "Export Chat" button in the side menu to download
  • Clear chat history using the delete button
  • Start a new conversation anytime

Note: Exported chats are saved as text files for easy reference.

What should I do if Oria isn't responding? expand_more

If you experience issues:

  1. Check your internet connection
  2. Refresh the page
  3. Clear your browser cache
  4. Try switching to a different version
  5. Report the issue through the feedback form

If problems persist, include your device info when reporting the issue.

How do I create an account with Oria? expand_more

Creating an account is simple:

  1. When you first visit Oria, a signup popup will appear
  2. Enter your name and email address
  3. Upload a profile picture (optional)
  4. Click "Create Account"

Your account information is stored locally and helps personalize your experience.

Can I use Oria on mobile devices? expand_more

Yes! Oria is fully responsive and works on:

  • Smartphones (iOS and Android)
  • Tablets
  • Desktop computers
  • Laptops

The interface automatically adjusts to your screen size for the best experience.

How do I switch between light and dark themes? expand_more

To change the theme:

  1. Click the settings icon in the side panel
  2. Find the "Dark Mode" toggle
  3. Switch it on for dark theme or off for light theme

Your theme preference will be saved for future sessions.

How does voice response work? expand_more

Voice response allows Oria to read responses aloud:

  1. Enable voice response in settings
  2. Ask a question as usual
  3. Oria will speak the response while displaying the text
  4. Adjust your device volume as needed

This feature is great for accessibility or when you're multitasking.

Is my data private when using Oria? expand_more

We take privacy seriously:

  • Your conversations are stored locally on your device
  • We don't permanently store your chat history on our servers
  • Your account information is used only for personalization
  • You can delete your data at any time

For sensitive information, we recommend starting a new chat after discussing confidential topics.

How do I edit my profile information? expand_more

To update your profile:

  1. Open the settings menu
  2. Your profile information appears at the top
  3. Click the edit icon next to your name to change it
  4. Click the edit icon on your profile picture to update it
  5. Email addresses cannot be changed after account creation

Your updated information will be saved automatically.

Can I use Oria offline? expand_more

Oria requires an internet connection to function:

  • All AI processing happens on our servers
  • You need to be online to send and receive messages
  • Previously loaded conversations may be viewable offline
  • Full functionality requires a stable internet connection

We're exploring limited offline capabilities for future updates.

How do I clear my chat history? expand_more

To clear your chat history:

  1. Click the delete button at the bottom of the chat
  2. Confirm that you want to clear the conversation
  3. Your chat will be reset and a new conversation will begin

Alternatively, you can click "New Chat" in the side panel to start fresh.

What file types can I upload to Oria? expand_more

Oria supports various file types:

  • Images: JPG, PNG, GIF, WebP
  • Documents: PDF, TXT, MD
  • Code: Various programming language files

File size limits apply (typically up to 10MB per file).

How accurate is Oria's information? expand_more

Oria strives for accuracy but has limitations:

  • Information is based on training data with a cutoff date
  • It may not have knowledge of very recent events
  • Technical information is generally reliable but should be verified
  • For critical applications, always double-check information

If you notice inaccuracies, please provide feedback to help us improve.

Can I share my Oria conversations with others? expand_more

Yes, you can share conversations in several ways:

  1. Export the chat as a text file and share it
  2. Take screenshots of important responses
  3. Copy and paste text from the conversation

When sharing, remember that the conversation contains both your inputs and Oria's responses.

What should I do if I find inappropriate content? expand_more

If you encounter inappropriate content:

  1. Use the feedback form to report the issue
  2. Include details about what happened
  3. Specify the question that triggered the response
  4. Our team will investigate and improve our filters

Oria is designed to be helpful and respectful, so we take these reports seriously.

  • Gmail (@gmail.com)

How does Advanced Mode work? expand_more

Advanced Mode changes how Oria responds:

  • Provides more focused and professional responses
  • Gives direct answers without unnecessary explanations
  • Uses technical language when appropriate
  • Available in XSS 4Q version
  • Toggle through the version popup menu

Best suited for experienced users who need precise information.

Why is voice response only available on PC? expand_more

Voice response is PC-only for several reasons:

  • Better performance and reliability on desktop browsers
  • More consistent voice synthesis quality
  • Reduced mobile data usage
  • Better integration with desktop audio systems
  • Mobile devices often have built-in text-to-speech options

We're working on optimizing voice features for mobile in future updates.

What's the difference between XSS and XQS versions? expand_more

The version differences are:

  • XSS (Speed Series):
    • Faster response times
    • Optimized for quick interactions
    • Best for real-time assistance
  • XQS (Quality Series):
    • More detailed responses
    • Enhanced accuracy
    • Better for complex tasks

Choose based on your priority: speed or detail.

How do I use keyboard shortcuts with Oria? expand_more

Available keyboard shortcuts:

  • Enter: Send message
  • Shift + Enter: Add new line in message
  • Esc: Close popups
  • Ctrl/Cmd + C: Copy selected text
  • Ctrl/Cmd + V: Paste content

These shortcuts help you interact with Oria more efficiently.
